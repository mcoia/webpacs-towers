By opting in to Reading History you understand that a record of your checked out materials will be stored on the library catalog. The library staff does not have access to your Reading History, however, it is subject to all applicable local, state, and federal laws, and under those laws, could be examined by law enforcement authorities without your permission. If this is of concern to you, you should not use the Reading History feature.
To opt in to Reading History, click “My Reading History” and click the “Opt In” link. To Opt Out, select “My Reading History” and click the “Opt Out” link. Once an item is deleted from a Reading History, it can not be retrieved. MOBIUS items only display in Reading History while checked out.

To renew item(s), click the “items currently checked out” link, place a check-mark by item(s) to renew, and select “RENEW SELECTED ITEMS” button.
